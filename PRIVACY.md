# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 11, 2026

## Overview

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy describes how the **Wikipedia Reader** application ("the App") handles your data.

## Information We Collect

We want to make this simple: **we do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information or usage data to our servers.** 

- **Local Storage:** The App may store certain preferences locally on your device (such as reading history, layout, or theme settings) to enhance your user experience. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developers.
- **Downloaded Content:** Any PDF articles you export using the App are saved directly to your local computer storage. 
- **Wikipedia Data:** The App retrieves content directly from Wikipedia's public API to display articles. This interaction is subject to the [Wikimedia Foundation Privacy Policy](https://foundation.wikimedia.org/wiki/Policy:Privacy_policy).

## Third-Party Services

The App interacts directly with Wikipedia to fetch article content. No other third-party tracking, analytics, or data collection services are integrated into this application.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update the App in the future to introduce features that might interact with your data differently, we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly.

## Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please submit an issue on the [project's official GitHub repository](https://github.com/kabirpathshala/wikipedia-kindle/issues).
